#Apps4Charity

Meldium is a startup emerging from Y Combinator and we wanted to find a way to do what we do best (build software) and contribute to charity in the process. Meldium helps businesses manage their long list of passwords & accounts on the web. Our customers ask for [lots of apps] so we decided to spend 24 hours in a race to add as many apps as we can. For every app we integrate, we'll donate $10 to [Watsi], a charity we love. If you request an app below, we'll double the bounty to $20!

32 apps added so far. 13h41m remaining. $680 donation.

Suggest an app to double our pledge: Tweet #Apps4Charity
